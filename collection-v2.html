LEGEND SERIES

The KG1 LEGEND SERIES uses a flat centered blank wheel as it’s platform. The primary focus of this forging is it’s pronounced wider lip and deeper look. Our combination of durability and unique design is sure to enhance the overall look of your specialty vehicle.

CONCAVE SERIES

The KG1 CONCAVE SERIES uses concave blank forgings as it’s multi-dimensional platform. They’re the perfect balance between width of lip and concave recessed depth, making the overall visual effect of the design more alluring for your specialty vehicle.

DUALLY SERIES

The KG1 DUALLY SERIES forging allows for optional 8 or 10 lug bolt hole design. This option can also give an 8 lug dually a rugged 10 lug look, or maximize the aspect of the wheel design in 8 lug direct drill design. At KG1, the choice is yours to complement the overall look of your custom dually setup.

SPORT SERIES

The KG1 SPORT SERIES is made for the performance street truck or SUV fanatic. This high offset forged design concept was aimed at non-lifted applications that desire a very strong and lightweight wheel fitment.

OVERLAND SERIES

The KG1 OVERLAND SERIES is a new line up of forged wheels for the most serious of off-road enthusiasts. While even in the most extreme terrain, they’re designed and engineered to withstand the pressure. With options of both TRUE and FAUX beedlock designs , the OVERLAND SERIES wheels have the quality and durabilty to suit any off-road enthusiast’s needs.
